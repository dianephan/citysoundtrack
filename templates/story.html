Your Life is a Movie

Inspired by my love of movie soundtracks and adventures traveling around the world, I wanted to create a map that encourages you to have a personalized soundtrack and feel like the main character.
It's a pleasant feeling romanticizing your life and listening to a song on a train, or reminiscing about the past when a certain song plays.

It's also fun watching movies that took place in the same city as you.

In another version of this app, I plotted a song from a movie that took place at the real location of the movie. Please reach out if you would like to contribute to this project and help populate the map with songs of movies that take place in your favorite cities.

Thank you for your stories and the music that means something to you! <3
